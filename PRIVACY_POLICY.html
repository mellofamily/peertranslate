# Privacy Policy for Peer Translate Effective Date: April 30, 2026 **Peer Translate** we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application. ## 1. Information We Collect Peer Translate is designed as an **on-device** translation tool. * **Audio Data:** We use the Microphone permission (`RECORD_AUDIO`) to capture your voice for the purpose of translation. * **On-Device Processing:** All audio captured is processed locally on your device using on-device AI models. We do **not** send your voice recordings to our servers or any third-party cloud services for processing. * **No Personal Information:** We do not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. ## 2. Permissions Used * **Microphone (`RECORD_AUDIO`):** Required to capture audio for translation. * **Internet (`INTERNET` & `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`):** Used solely to download the necessary AI models and updates from Google services. No user data is uploaded. * **Notifications (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`):** Used to show progress for model downloads. ## 3. Data Storage The app does not maintain a permanent database of your translations on our servers. Any translation history is stored locally on your device and can be cleared by you at any time within the app settings. ## 4. Third-Party Services We use Google Play Services for app distribution and Firebase for basic, anonymous analytics (if enabled) to improve app stability. These services may collect information as described in Google's Privacy Policy. ## 5. Changes to This Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. ## 6.
